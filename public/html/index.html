Turn on the Music

Whether you're a venue or a band, we can get get you hooked up.

Having worked as both a musician and a bartender, I know communication between venues and bands can be stressful. Venues are required to reach out to different bands on a monthly basis to fill their calendar and must cruise Facebook to find new entertainment they would like to hire. Musicians must email, call, or literally drive by venues to advertise themselves and give the venue their availability. Booking agents have been a solution in the past but generally take huge cuts from both sides and some are just not reliable. As a band I would like the capability to advertise style of music, contact info, and availability and browse several venues in one place to contact them for potential gigs. As a venue I would like one place to browse several bands in my area and see their availability, rates, and musical style without having to cruise Facebook trying to find talent. Enter Rapsheet, a one stop app where bands and venues can sign up for an account and join an online music community to simplify the booking process.
